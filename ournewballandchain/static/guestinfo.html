Kate and Jonathan's Favorite Restaurants and Frequented Watering-Holes

  • San Francisco Coffee: Jonathan's favorite! Great for a tasty caffinated pick-me up.
  • Fox Brothers BBQ : Kate's favorite BBQ! Relaxed atmosphere and tasty southern fare. Don't miss the Brisket, Pulled Pork, Cole Slaw, Mac'n'cheese, Brunswick stew, Chocolate Pecan Pie... so, basically don't miss any of it. It's not to be recommended for vegetarians - mostly meats available.
  • Highland Bakery : Jonathan's #1 brunch spot. We love the home-cooked feel! Hoppin' Highland, Shrimp'n'Grits, Burrito de Huevos, Fried Chicken Benedict, yum. Three locations and bakeshops - Old 4th Ward, Midtown, and Buckhead.
  • The Porter Beer Bar : This is a must-visit every time we're in Atlanta! Amazing beer list inlcuding local artisanal and good ol'favorites. A tasty food menu to compliments your brew of choice. The staff and servers here take their drink/food duties really seriously. Any questions, they'll help you out.
  • The Bookhouse Pub : A cozy, tride'n'true pub. Inexpensive beer, some interesting cocktails. Recommended : Chili, Chorizo burger, Veggie Burger.
  • Ria's Bluebird : A great brunch option! Full of cheeky character. Chef Ria won the Food Network program ""Chopped"!
  • Mary Mac's Tea Room : Sweet and quite old-fashioned Southern.
  • The Midway Pub
  • Farm Burger
  • The Varsity
  • Wrecking Bar
